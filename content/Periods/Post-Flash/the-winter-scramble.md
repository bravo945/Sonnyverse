# The Winter Scramble (2044-2050s)

> *The following text is prologue from Project Epoch lore, prior to the merge with Sonnyverse.*

## Overview

The Winter Scramble was a mass migration phenomenon during the early Nuclear Winter period (early 2040s to early 2050s) that fundamentally shaped the USCPF's development and the balance of power among post-Flash American factions.

## The Choice

Survivors of the nuclear war faced two options:
1. **Settle locally** - Huddle with nearby survivors for immediate survival
2. **Migrate** - Risk travel to find established settlements

During the hopeless early days of Nuclear Winter, favorable survival groups were scarce outside the **CDF's coalition**. When word spread of the United States' resurgence through the **USCPF**—via new radio signals or direct sightings—**millions immediately flocked toward New England**.

## Impact on USCPF

This natural "blitzkrieg" migration:
- **Shaped the USCPF's demographic foundation**, granting massive population numbers
- **Enabled mandatory conscription**, creating a military force capable of competing with multiple major factions simultaneously
- **Created severe strain** on the fledgling government

## Consequences

### Immediate Challenges
The United States was **unprepared for the instant influx**. During Nuclear Winter conditions, the USCPF struggled to:
- Feed the population
- Provide shelter
- Maintain civil order

### Strategic Setbacks
This crisis **delayed the country's primary reformatory objective by nearly 10 years** compared to the original timeline, allowing:
- **CDF** to grow into an enormous territorial power
- **SLF** (Southwest Libertarian Front) to fortify their West Coast foothold

### Migration Patterns
Harsh post-war conditions meant survivors "weren't too picky" when seeking homes. Travel already being high-risk, **pit-stops like CDF scraptowns became permanent settlements** in regions farther from New England.
