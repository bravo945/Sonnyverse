# Wasteland Mercantilism

The hellish wastelands of Post-Flash America isolated once bustling cities from each other, making the shortest walks through city blocks a dangerous and uncertain venture. Wastelanders adept enough to travel the ruined roads found purpose and profit in merchant-ship, connecting islands of survivors with both the commodities they carried and the knowledge they shared.
