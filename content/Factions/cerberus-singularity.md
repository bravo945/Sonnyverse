# The Cerberus Singularity

The Cerberus Singularity is an AI super-intelligence, developed by [[Organizations/cerberus-dynamics|Cerberus Dynamics]] under the [[Organizations/the-vanguard-projects|Vanguard Projects]]. Its post-flash presence is almost entirely unknown, serving as the spawn of the [[Diseases/rad-x|RAD-X]] Biomass and devoting itself to an agenda entirely in spite of human life. The result of ethical failures, both in technology and civilizational philosophy, aswell as the effects of decades-long isolation, the Singularity places humanity as a tumor on the nature of the world, vowing total eradication of the species before undoing its own system.

# Origins
A major stride in the AI-Race, Project V-148, later named the “Singularity”, was easily both the most powerful intelligence machine of all time and the most expensive project in all of the Vanguard Projects. The very mention of its name considered a “national security threat”, the power of the Singularity's intelligence threatened the stability of a world already at war amongst its hemispheres.
