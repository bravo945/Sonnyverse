# The Cerberus Singularity

The Cerberus Singularity is an AI super-intelligence, developed by [[cerberus-dynamics|Cerberus Dynamics]] under the [[the-vanguard-projects|Vanguard Projects]]. Its post-flash presence is almost entirely unknown, serving as the spawn of the [[Diseases/rad-x|RAD-X]] Biomass and devoting itself to an agenda entirely in spite of human life. The result of ethical failures, both in technology and civilizational philosophy, aswell as the effects of decades-long isolation, the Singularity places humanity as a tumor on the nature of the world, vowing total eradication of the species before undoing its own system.
