# Faction Overview Format
The Faction Overview Format is for organizing quick and trivial faction info to the top of a respective faction's document for time-saving and convenience.

# Policies and Sub-Formatting
For quick reference, the faction overview will sit under the Faction Name, Prioritized as `H1`(First Header). Following Headers should not be contained under the faction name header, they should have their own containing headers to make finding the FO in an overview tab more convenient. 
## Dates
Many factions' formation and dissolution are chronologically vague and convoluted, and it's not often that a faction holds an official date of either. Therefore, most dates of such nature will be approximated based on the month or year, fore-mentioned by a  `~` (tilde) symbol.

If a dissolution date is unwritten yet, the dissolution date will be `TBW` (To Be Written. If the date of dissolution is out of scope of the wiki's relevant timeline, the dissolution date will be `Beyond`. If information is intentionally withheld, then the dissolution date will be `Unknown`.

# Faction Nature Index
The faction nature index is a category under the FOF that attributes 4 qualitative spectrums to desvcribe 
## Qualities
> Adjectives summarizing the attitude of the faction

- Chaotic
- Governmental
- Commercial
- Syndicated
- Criminal
- Civil-Service
- Militant
- Tribal
## Priorities
> General priorities and objectives behind the faction

- Chaos
- Civil Order
- Profit
- Conquest
- Benevolence
- Malevolence
- Rescue
- Preservation
- Spite (of opposing force)
## Structure
> The general organization of the faction

- Commune
- Community
- Coalition
- Oligarchy
- Autocracy
- Authoritarian
- Political Party
- Ideological Movement
- Confederacy
- Federation
- Armed Force
- Anarchy
- Posse
- Family
- Dynasty
## Integrity
> The cohesion and socio-political definition of the faction

- Amorphous
	- No structure or official stature; faction is completely defined by its orientation.
- Conflicted
	- Faction consists of conflicting interests under the same socio-political structure.
- Vaguely Oriented
	- Faction consists of vague and unofficial structures, focused more on orientation than morphage.
- Collective
	- Faction is a decentralized organization of incremental community(s).
- Cohesive
	- Faction is a clearly-defined centralized force.
- Legalist
	- Faction is a legal/governmental entity with defined laws, territory, and jurisdiction.
- Diplomatic
	- Faction is a recognized institute by other official entities and legalist factions.
- Hive-mind
	- Faction is a psychologically-orchestrated culture of people with minimal agency.
- Collective Conscious
	- Reserved for AI Installments
	- Faction is a single entity of devices or avatars.
