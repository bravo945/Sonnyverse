# OOC Notes
## Condition
In this universe, up until the Reconstruction era, the USCPF as a territory barely expands passed the New England, Mid-Atlantic, and Appohzarkan regions. The reclamation plan prioritizes major cities and pre-flash population centers over general swathes of land, and until the USCPF establishes a clear geographical network/road-map through the borders of a state, the rest of that state remains lawless.

# (USCPF) United States Civilization Preservation Force

![[uscpf-overview]]

The USCPF (United States Civilization Preservation Force) is the post-flash successor of the United States Armed Forces under the executive [[usdp|Department of Preservation]].

After the Preservation Act, all acting entities, technology, and personnel under the US military are indefinitely under the jurisdiction of the USCPF and its leading Cabal (non-voluntarily).
