# The Market

![[the-market-overview]]

The market is a loose militia encompassing most trade around the Halfmoon region.

 The most recognizable sub-faction in southern Post Flash America during the [Nuclear Winter] era was the White Star.
 
![[market-symbol.png]]

A key example of the [Market Defense Algorithm](Projects/Writing/Sonnyverse/repo/Sonnyverse/content/Periods/Post-Flash/post-flash.md), the Market is a decentralized band of militiamen belonging to different shanty-towns across the region. Their goal is to help defend and regulate trade, establish trade routes and defend merchants from bandits and other hostile-groups.

Each region the market inhabits has their own division with specific insignia and different agendas, with Halfmoon under the infamous White Star Division. Only named for the graffiti they draw, anywhere with their insignia is either a warning, or a way home.

A mercenary group of sorts, the White Star mostly acts as hired help; defending shanty towns, taking raider bases, and doing odd-jobs around the region.

Though the faction has good intentions, it is way too ambitious with the resources it has. The only insignia on most members is an armband of ripped (blue) denim. As for weaponry, most are expected to bring their guns from home, which ends up with most of the faction using broken chair legs and scavenged weaponry.

Being a vital piece of the economy, their faction obviously puts trade above all else, meaning if your town isn't vital or close to a trade route, the cost of having the White Star's presence is insanely high. Partnerships are only with the rich, as the way of the market goes.
