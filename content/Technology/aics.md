# (ACIS) Artificially Intelligent Combat System

The Artificially Intelligent Combat System was a project under the umbrella of [the Vanguard Projects](https://discord.com/channels/1270266346743136286/1283517376859738132), which included a wide range of adaptive networks and military products designed to fortify and protect points-of-interest behind the frontlines of WWIII.

# Components
## UTA
Unmanned Terrestrial Agents, or UTA's for short, were a form of Combat AI developed by the AICS to be deployed into numerous battlefields, working with networks and human-troops to decimate enemy forces. The most common of these UTA's was the Model 9, an anthropomorphic, simpler minded, and easy to produce model equipped with the standard rifleman loadout and a camo paint appropriate to the environment around them.

UTA's often moved in groups, serving as scouts for human troops. The majority of platoons were Model 9's 
## Networks
Especially from the mid 2030's and onward, AICS products would become entire AI installments, as opposed to individualistic combat unit AI. Each combat unit would think together as a hivemind, communicating to each other and the servers at the base in real time. Each network was protected with military-grade cyber security, which would later be rendered obsolete by enemy hacker groups. These networks could operate platoons of UTA's, UAV's, and automatic artillery fire at the same time. Some networks were even cloud based, sacrificing security for unfathomably large areas for the network to inhabit.
