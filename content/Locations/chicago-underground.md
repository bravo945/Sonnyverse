# Chicago Underground
The Chicago Underground is a collective of underground maintenance tunnels, sewers, and subways navigated and inhabited by a network survivors from the Chicago Flash.

# History
## Pre-Flash
The Chicago Underground, growing ever-faster in space by industrial and data infrastructure, aswell as in numbers from the massive unemployment rates brought by [[Technology/big-boom|big-boom]] automation, formed its first tunnels long before the Flash. Homeless Unions throughout the underground walked as predecessors to the sticks
