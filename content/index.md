# Summary

Sonnyverse is my collective take on the After the Flash universe and its worldbuilding.

As a long-time roleplayer in the series, I'm very fond of the atmosphere, settings, factions, and elements that help compose the game and its story. However, over the years, I've gotten nit-picky about how things turned out in the series, the main problems I have being:
- The setting is getting old and less connected to reality. Though nostalgic in retrospect, its realistic and "plausible" factors are too dated to be taken seriously.
- The series is nearly 20 years old, and has watched its orientation grow up from a teenager's goofy side-project to a serious source of income. The games have properly reflected their focus over time, but later installments have been burdened by past decisions, ignorant of gameplay and rich story-telling.
- The influx of youthful and unskilled roleplayers in the ATF community has made the environment more toxic and alien to the series, especially after the release of Wintertide. Because of this, average unguided roleplay has become near impossible to attain.
- The original core ATF factions, especially the CDF, SEAL, DECORP (etc), are unnecessarily everywhere and because of that have stripped the game of more potential unique factions. ATF doesn't need entirely the same factions to keep its core charm and gameplay.

## My Goal

I do not have the time, energy, nor legal clearance to make my own collective spinoff with enough detail, players, and story. I don't expect to. However, the passion I do have for writing and worldbuilding is enough to atleast conceive a universe that I'm satisfied with.
The main things I want to focus on is making a universe that:
- Properly addresses the political and technological themes of the 2020's
- Filters lore elements for an interesting story template, both in conception and gameplay
- Fosters a community of kind and similarly passionate people
- Gives variety and decoration to both the factions and timeline
